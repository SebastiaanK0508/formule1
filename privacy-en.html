Privacy Policy

This privacy policy describes how your personal data is collected, used, and shared when you visit or use the Webbair Formula 1 website. We value your privacy and ensure that the personal data you share with us is handled carefully and confidentially.

1. Data Collected

We collect two types of data:

  • Automatically Collected Data: When you visit the Website, we automatically collect certain information about your device, such as your IP address, browser type, internet provider, and the pages you view. This information is collected via cookies and log files and is used for the administration and security of the Website and for analytical purposes to improve the user experience.
  • Data You Provide: This is data you voluntarily provide to us, for example, through contact forms or other interactions on the site. This includes your name and email address.

2. Use of Your Data

The collected data is used for the following purposes:

  • To maintain and improve the Website.
  • For analyzing website usage and behavior, so we can optimize our content.
  • To respond to your questions and requests via contact forms.
  • For security and fraud prevention.

3. Sharing Your Data

We will not sell, rent, or otherwise disclose your personal data to third parties unless:

  • You have given us explicit permission to do so.
  • It is necessary for the provision of our services (for example, to hosting providers).
  • We are legally required to do so.

4. Cookies

The Website uses cookies to improve the user experience. Cookies are small text files placed on your computer or mobile device. For example, they help us to make the website function better and to collect anonymized statistics about website visits. You can adjust your browser settings to refuse cookies, but this may affect the functionality of the Website.

5. Your Rights

In accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), you have several rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right to access: You can submit a request to see what personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to rectification: You can ask us to correct incorrect or incomplete data.
  • Right to erasure: You have the right to have your data erased unless there is a legal basis to retain it.
  • Right to object: You can object to the processing of your data.

To exercise any of these rights, you can contact us using the contact details provided at the bottom of this policy.

6. Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be changed from time to time. We recommend that you visit this page regularly to stay informed of any changes. Changes will take effect as soon as they are posted on the Website.

7. Contact

For questions about this privacy policy or your personal data, you can contact Webbair via the contact form on our website.
